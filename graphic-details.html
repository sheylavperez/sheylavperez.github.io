Aquatic Harmony: Fish and Dolphin Logo Design

This logo design features a harmonious blend of a fish and a dolphin, symbolizing the unity and diversity of marine life. The design employs a split-complementary color scheme, combining a base color with two adjacent colors to create visual interest and balance.

My Thought Process:

Tools used: Adobe Illustrator: Utilized for vector illustration, allowing precise control over shapes and colors. Color Wheel: Employed to select and harmonize colors effectively.
