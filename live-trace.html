Project information

  • Project Title:Pixel Art Tribute: Singer Portrait"
  • Category: Digital Art - Pixel Art
  • Subject: Musical Inspiration
  • Software: Adobe Photoshop

Overview:

My recent project combines the nostalgic charm of pixel art with the practicality of merchandise, resulting in a unique fusion of digital art and physical products. Inspired by my love for music and artistic expression, I created a pixel art design featuring a singer, which I then transferred onto a tote bag. This project showcases the versatility of pixel art beyond digital screens, transforming it into a wearable piece of functional art.
