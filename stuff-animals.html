Project information

  • Project Title: Grid-Based Magazine Layout Redesign
  • Category: Graphic Design Composition and layout
  • Subject: Design principles and Elements
  • Software: Adobe InDesign

Concept:

Stuffed animals, often cherished companions in childhood and beyond, hold a significant place in the fabric of human society. This theoretical framework seeks to explore the multifaceted nature of stuffed animals, delving into their historical, psychological, cultural, and economic dimensions. By examining the role of stuffed animals through various theoretical lenses, we aim to gain a deeper understanding of their significance and impact on individuals and society as a whole.

Layout Variations:

  • Use of mirrored poses and facial expressions to create a sense of harmony and reflection.
  • Introduction of asymmetrical details, such as the arrow motif on the bunny, to add visual interest and draw attention to specific elements.

A symmetrical design and an arrow bunny concepts

In my designs, I explore a variety of layout techniques to infuse personality and charm into each stuffed animal:

Layout Variations:

  • Contrasting colors and textures to create a sense of eerie beauty.
  • Distorted proportions and asymmetrical compositions to evoke a sense of unease.
  • Incorporation of occult symbols and motifs to add layers of mystery and intrigue.
  • Subtle hints of decay and wear to imbue each stuffed animal with a haunting sense of history.
