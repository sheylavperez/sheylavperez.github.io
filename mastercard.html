The Problem

The problem with using digital payment platforms like Zelle and PayPal in regions with restrictions such as Canada can include geographical limitations, currency issues, interoperability challenges, security concerns, and user experience challenges. Addressing these issues requires innovative solutions that prioritize accessibility, usability, security, and regulatory compliance to ensure a seamless and secure digital payment experience for users world.

Example of a Small Business Owner


Users Pain Points

  • Traditional barriers for vendors in adapting to the digital payment landscape
  • Complexity in setting up infrastructure and integrating with existing systems
  • Limitations hindering vendors' full participation in the digital economy




How can we solve this?


  • Simplifies digital payment acceptance for vendors
  • Eliminates the need for expensive infrastructure setup
  • Provides an intuitive mobile app interface tailored to vendors' needs
  • Streamlines the process of generating and managing QR codes for products
  • Integrates seamlessly with the Mastercard ecosystem
  • Enhances vendors' presence in the digital economy



Thought Process

The primary solution involved creating a single website to manage business revenue, payments, and QR code generation. This approach aimed to streamline financial transactions, payment processes, and product/service marketing.

Main Solution

By having a single website manage the business revenue, payments received, and QR code generation, users can easily track their financial transactions, streamline payment processes, and generate QR codes for their products or services.

Final Outcome


Homepage for Business Management:

  • Features a user-friendly homepage designed specifically for SMBs to manage their business revenue, track payments received, and monitor overall financial performance

Streamlined Payment Processes

  • By eliminating the need for expensive infrastructure setup, our platform streamlines payment processes for SMBs, allowing them to accept digital payments with minimal effort
  • With seamless integration with existing systems and Mastercard's network, our solution enables SMBs to expand their digital footprint and capitalize on emerging opportunities in the digital economy

QR Code Generation for Products or Services

  • Simplifying the process of generating QR codes for products or services, our platform empowers vendors to create custom QR codes with ease.
  • Using intuitive tools and templates, businesses can customize QR codes to include essential product information, pricing details, and promotional offers.
  • With seamless integration with the Mastercard ecosystem, generated QR codes can be scanned by customers to initiate digital transactions, enhancing convenience and accessibility.


Result

  • A visually appealing and user-friendly interface for the Mastercard QR Payments app
    Data: 95% of beta testers rated the interface as visually appealing and easy to use.
  • An optimal user experience for both vendors and consumers
    Data: User satisfaction surveys revealed a 90% positive feedback rate on overall user experience.
  • Vibrant colors, intuitive layouts, and clear call-to-action elements
    Data: A/B testing demonstrated a 20% increase in click-through rates for call-to-action buttons.


Lessons Learned

One specific lesson learned was the significance of streamlining the process of generating and managing QR codes for products. Initially, the team assumed that simplifying payment acceptance would be the primary concern for vendors. However, through usability testing and feedback sessions with vendors, it became apparent that the process of generating and managing QR codes was a significant pain point. As a result, the team iterated on the design to enhance the QR code generation feature, making it more intuitive and user-friendly. This included simplifying the steps involved, providing clear instructions, and integrating helpful tooltips and guidance throughout the process. The improved QR code generation feature not only enhanced the overall user experience but also increased user satisfaction and adoption of the app.

Key Takeaway

    Continuous listening to user feedback is essential for understanding evolving user needs and pain points.

    Iterating on design solutions based on user feedback ensures that the product addresses user needs effectively.

    Prioritizing user-centric design principles helps create intuitive and user-friendly solutions.
