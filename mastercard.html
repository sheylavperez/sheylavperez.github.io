Empowering SMBs: Mastercard's QR Payments Revolutionizes Digital Transactions

Summary:

The primary goal is to simplify digital transactions, eliminate the need for vendor infrastructure, and seamlessly integrate businesses into the Mastercard ecosystem.

Role

The following roles designing this app:

  • User Experience (UX) Designer
  • Product Management

Details

Duration: 6 weeks

Tools:

  • Figma
  • Miro
  • Google Slides
  • Balsamiq

Deliverables

High-fidelity interactive prototype

UX/UI Design:

  • Competitive analysis
  • Personas
  • High-fidelity mockups and prototypes

The Problem

The problem with using digital payment platforms like Zelle and PayPal in regions with restrictions such as Canada can include geographical limitations, currency issues, interoperability challenges, security concerns, and user experience challenges. Addressing these issues requires innovative solutions that prioritize accessibility, usability, security, and regulatory compliance to ensure a seamless and secure digital payment experience for users world.

Qualitative Research

User Interview Example

Interviewee: Sarah Lopez, Owner of Café Verde (Small Coffee Shop in Toronto, Canada)

Key Insight:

" I can't afford to set up a website where customers can pay directly, so I’m left with limited options to accept digital payments."

Takeaway:

SMBs need an affordable, easy-to-implement solution that eliminates reliance on expensive infrastructure.

Persona: Small Business Owner

User's Pain Points

  • Traditional barriers for vendors in adapting to the digital payment landscape
  • Complexity in setting up infrastructure and integrating with existing systems
  • Limitations hindering vendors' full participation in the digital economy

How can we solve this?

Payment Solutions for SMBs:

Core Insight:

Small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs) need affordable, easy-to-implement payment systems.

Solution:

We will provide SMBs with a mobile app interface that simplifies digital payment acceptance, eliminating the need for expensive infrastructure setup. This interface will make it easy for businesses to accept payments instantly, without requiring complex or costly setups.

Seamless Integration with Existing Systems:

Core Insight:

SMBs want solutions that work with their current systems without disruption.

Solution:

We will ensure that our platform integrates seamlessly with the Mastercard ecosystem, enabling businesses to manage their transactions, track sales, and generate QR codes without switching to new systems. This will enhance vendor efficiency and facilitate a smooth transition into the digital economy.

Enhanced Vendor Visibility:

Core Insight:

SMBs need increased visibility to thrive in a competitive digital marketplace.

Solution:

By enhancing the vendors' presence in the digital economy, we will empower small businesses to grow and reach new customers. The solution will allow vendors to expand their digital footprint, creating new opportunities for growth and connecting with customers.

The Iteration Journey

The primary solution involved creating a single website to manage business revenue, payments, and QR code generation. This approach aimed to streamline financial transactions, payment processes, and product/service marketing.

Final Solution

By having a single website manage the business revenue, payments received, and QR code generation, users can easily track their financial transactions, streamline payment processes, and generate QR codes for their products or services..

Final Interface

Design of the career description interface, featuring progress bars to track career exploration. Users can visually assess their progress in exploring different career paths.

By eliminating the need for expensive infrastructure setup, our platform streamlines payment processes for SMBs, allowing them to accept digital payments with minimal effort

With seamless integration with existing systems and Mastercard's network, our solution enables SMBs to expand their digital footprint and capitalize on emerging opportunities in the digital economy

Simplifying the process of generating QR codes for products or services, our platform empowers vendors to create custom QR codes with ease.

Using intuitive tools and templates, businesses can customize QR codes to include essential product information, pricing details, and promotional offers.

With seamless integration with the Mastercard ecosystem, generated QR codes can be scanned by customers to initiate digital transactions, enhancing convenience and accessibility.

Impact That Speaks Volumes

6-Month Pilot Results

95% of beta testers rated the interface as visually appealing and easy to use.

90% positive feedback rate on overall user experience based on surveys.

20% increase in click-through rates for call-to-action buttons through A/B testing.

Lessons from the Trenches

Improved Usability Through Clear Guidance

We simplified the steps involved, provided clear instructions, and integrated helpful tooltips and guidance throughout the process. This approach not only improved the user experience but also significantly boosted user satisfaction.

Increased Adoption Through a Better Experience

By focusing on user feedback and making the QR code generation process easier to navigate, the team successfully improved the overall user experience, which resulted in increased app adoption by vendors.

Key Takeaway

Continuous listening to user feedback is essential for understanding evolving user needs and pain points.

Iterating on design solutions based on user feedback ensures that the product addresses user needs effectively.

Prioritizing user-centric design principles helps create intuitive and user-friendly solutions.

Closing Scene

Reflecting on the success of this project, the importance of understanding and addressing the real, underlying needs of users became clearer than ever. It reinforced the notion that a design rooted in empathy and constant iteration is key to creating solutions that truly make a difference.
