Project Overview

As a first-generation college student, I understood the confusion many students face when trying to choose a major or career path. This inspired me to lead the design and development of a Career Services website for Queens College â€” a platform that simplifies career exploration through engaging, interactive features. I worked with a team of five to build the product from the ground up, collaborating closely with engineers and student advisors. Our goal was to make career information more accessible, especially for students from underrepresented backgrounds. .

The site empowers students to explore careers aligned with their strengths and interests through personalized guidance, job simulations, and dynamic tools. The result is an inclusive, student-centered experience that supports informed, confident decision-making about the future.

The Challenge

  • Outdated user interface with confusing navigation and product categorization

  • Poor mobile experience with high bounce rates on smartphones

  • Complex checkout process leading to 72% cart abandonment

  • Limited product filtering and search capabilities

  • Inefficient inventory management system for store administrators

Our Solution

  • Complete UX redesign with intuitive category structure and navigation

  • Mobile-first responsive design with optimized product browsing

  • Streamlined 2-step checkout process with multiple payment options

  • Advanced filtering system with AI-powered product recommendations

  • Custom admin dashboard with real-time analytics and inventory tracking

Our Process

Research

We conducted extensive user interviews, competitive analysis, and usability testing of the existing platform to identify pain points.

  • User interviews with 24 customers
  • Heatmap analysis of existing site
  • Competitive analysis of 8 retailers

Strategy

Based on research insights, we developed a comprehensive strategy focusing on user experience and conversion optimization.

  • User personas development
  • Customer journey mapping
  • Information architecture redesign

Design

We created wireframes and high-fidelity prototypes with a focus on simplicity, visual hierarchy, and conversion optimization.

  • Low-fidelity wireframing
  • Interactive prototyping
  • Visual design system creation

Development

We built a custom e-commerce solution with a focus on performance, security, and scalability for future growth.

  • Frontend development with React
  • Backend API development
  • Integration testing and deployment
