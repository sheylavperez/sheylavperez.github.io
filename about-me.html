My Story

Like many other immigrant stories, my parents came from Guatemala to New York for a better life, where I was born as the second oldest of six siblings. Growing up in a big, loving family, I learned the values of humility and gratitude.

I studied mathematics with a minor in computer science, using data-driven analysis and technical foundations to inform user research, guide product decisions, and uncover meaningful consumer insights.



Entrepreneur, Designer & STEM Mentor

I'm a multifaceted creative: a designer, entrepreneur, and educator passionate about empowering others. As a math and coding instructor, I nurture problem-solving skills, while my web design expertise brings ideas to life. I'm deeply committed to supporting my family and community, particularly advocating for Latinas in tech through organizations like Code as a Second Language and Girls Who Code. When I'm not coding or designing, you'll find me dancing, ice skating, or immersed in the world of Animal Crossing.
