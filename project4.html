Project Overview: Pet Health Connect - Enhancing Chewy Customer Experience

Name: Pet Health Connect

Duration: 3 months, 2022

Scope: App design, user experience improvement, and integration with Chewy website

Tools: Figma, Video Chat Integration, Chrome Extension Development

Introduction:

Pet Health Connect is a revolutionary app designed to elevate the customer experience for pet health product shopping on Chewy. Recognizing the challenges users face in finding expert opinions and suitable products, this app aims to provide a safe, quarantine-friendly solution. Through features like chat support, video consultations, and a dashboard of personalized recommendations, Pet Health Connect makes shopping for pet health products seamless and enjoyable.

Pet Health Connect Goals:

  1. Expert Recommendations:Empower Chewy personnel with the ability to provide expert recommendations, making the shopping experience more personalized and informed.
  2. User-Friendly Interface:Design an intuitive app interface allowing users to easily access expert advice, recommendations, and make purchases effortlessly.
  3. Chat Support: Design the app to streamline the payment process for vendors, allowing them to create, print, and manage QR codes for all products effortlessly.
  4. Video Consultations: ntegrate a video feature enabling users to have virtual consultations with experts for a more personalized and detailed shopping experience.
  1. Dashboard of Recommendations:Create a user-friendly dashboard displaying personalized product recommendations based on the pet's health needs and history.
  2. Pharmacy View:Develop a specialized view for pharmacy-related products, ensuring a seamless shopping experience for users looking for pet medications.
  3. Chrome Extension: Develop a Chrome extension allowing Chewy personnel to recommend items seamlessly while users browse the Chewy website.
  4. Manage User Recommendations: Implement a feature enabling Chewy personnel to manage and track user recommendations, ensuring a continuous and personalized shopping experience.

Results

Pet Health Connect promises to revolutionize the way users shop for pet health products on Chewy. With expert recommendations, interactive chat support, video consultations, and a user-friendly dashboard, the app aims to enhance the overall customer experience. The Chrome extension further streamlines the process, making product recommendations seamlessly integrated into the Chewy website. Stay tuned for the app's launch, where pet health shopping becomes not just convenient but also a personalized and expert-guided journey.
