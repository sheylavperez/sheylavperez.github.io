About Me

Born in New York to Guatemalan parents, I’m the second oldest of six and grew up in a tight-knit, hardworking family that taught me humility, resilience, and gratitude. Though I studied math and computer science, my true passion has always been design.

With 3+ years of experience in graphic design, I specialize in blending creativity and problem-solving to craft visual solutions that resonate. From branding to web design, I aim to create work that’s both beautiful and meaningful.

Outside of design, I’m inspired by art galleries, urban photography, and learning new creative techniques.

Creative Process

A methodical approach that ensures every project achieves its objectives while maintaining creative excellence.

Discovery

I begin by understanding your goals, audience, and vision. This phase involves research, competitor analysis, and gathering inspiration to inform the design direction.

1

Concept Development

I create initial concepts and sketches based on our discovery findings. This is where ideas begin to take shape and the creative direction starts to bloom.

2

Design Refinement

Based on your feedback, I refine the chosen concept, perfecting every detail. Colors, typography, imagery, and layout are all carefully considered and polished.

3

Implementation
