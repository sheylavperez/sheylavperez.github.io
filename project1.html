CareerDay Project Overview:

Name: CareerDay

Duration: 1 week, 2022

Scope: Product design, branding, and web design

Tools: Figma

Introduction:

I am a UX designer currently working on two exciting projects, CareerDay and CUNY Clash. For CareerDay, the goal is to empower students by simplifying complex career information in a way that appeals to Gen Z's love of gaming. As a founder, I am dedicated to building this product from the ground up, utilizing my skills in product design, branding, and web design with Figma as the primary tool.

CareerDay Goals:

  1. Simplicity and Readability: Ensure the information is simple and easy to read, catering to Gen Z preferences.
  2. Gamification Focus: Emphasize gamification features in the interface for an engaging user experience.
  3. Brand Development: Establish a strong and memorable brand identity for CareerDay.
  4. User-Friendly Experience: Design an app that is not only visually appealing but also offers a seamless and enjoyable user journey.

Key Components

  1. User Research: Conducted thorough user research to understand the needs and challenges of engineers in the context of CUNY Clash
  2. User Personas: Developed detailed user personas reflecting the diverse needs of engineers participating in CUNY Clash
  3. Wireframes: Created wireframes emphasizing essential features and functionalities

Results:

The UX designs for CareerDay and CUNY Clash have been meticulously crafted, combining creativity with user-centric principles. For CareerDay, the focus is on simplicity, gamification, brand development, and a user-friendly experience. For CUNY Clash, the emphasis is on a simplified format to help engineers achieve their goals, with a target of a perfect 10 in usability and satisfaction. Both projects reflect a commitment to excellence in user experience and continuous improvement. Stay tuned for the unveiling of the final products, where vision meets expertise in UX design.
